    Polar bears are starving.

    Sea ice is melting due to global warming. Polar bears become stranded on land where they are unable to hunt for their primary food source: seals.[1]

    Our Mission.

    Conserve the polar bear population and their respective environment. We work with other teams connected to the Arctic to ensure we have a positive effect.[2]

    Support the cause.

    Discover multiple methods we can take part in to help save the Arctic's polar bears.
