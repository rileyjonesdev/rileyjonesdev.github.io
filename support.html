    • Methods to support polar bears

    #1: Reduce power usage.

    Generating power results in carbon pollution, which is one of the largest contributors to climate change. [1] Climate change impacts the Arctic the most, resulting in melting sea ice.[2] If we all unplug appliances and turn off our lights, polar bears could see generations to come.

    #2: Fight the Willow Project.

    The Willow Project extracts oil and gas from Alaska, resulting in unleashing 260 million metric tons of CO₂ in the next 30 years.[3] The green-lit project is the nation's largest U.S. oil project yet.[4] This will not only pollute the air resulting in high global warming, but it'll destroy habitats for polar bears and other animals.[5]

    #3: Donate to the Polar Bear Crisis.

    We are proud to be non-profit. Any amount helps contribute to our mission of protecting polar bears for years to come. Your donation will provide funding for further research and more on-the-ground conservation efforts.
